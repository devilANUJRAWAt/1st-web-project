CSR Initiatives

DLF Foundation was established in 2008 as the CSR implementing arm of the DLF Group and works to deliver high impact interventions in the areas of education, healthcare and social sustainability.

About DLF Foundation

DLF Foundation's vision is to transform communities we work with through high impact interventions in spheres of education, healthcare, and social sustainability with the resolve of enabling a better quality of life backed by sustainable ecosystems for all stakeholders within the immediate and extended communities

We accomplish this through the creation of customized programmes and innovative institutions that address India’s current development challenges and by providing a platform for the Foundation to collaborate with other institutions, professionals and subject matter experts to deliver sustained, magnified impact across communities of interest.

DLF Foundation transforms communities by delivering high-impact interventions in the following focus areas:

DLF Foundation is committed to driving excellence along with inclusivity in education, helping shape the next generation of India. DLF Foundation’s flagship education Programme 'DLF CARES' was established with the premise that every child has the right to quality education and the primary objective is to nurture and handhold children to enable them to realize their true potential and become productive and responsible citizens of tomorrow. Under this Programme, we award scholarships to deserving students from low-income families and support their education in quality schools and institutions up to class XII and beyond. Since the inception of the program in 2011, close to 2000+ students from 19 states have been supported in their education and over 300 have gone on to pursue higher education. The scholars in addition to the academic interventions are also provided counselling and mentoring support through a dedicated Counselling cell established at the Foundation.

In addition to academics, DLF Foundation also believes in nurturing the leaders of tomorrow in the field of sports. In line with this vision, the Foundation set up the Golf Excellence Program in partnership with DLF Golf Academy in 2017 to encourage the next generation of golf champions in India. Over the last 4 years, the Golf Excellence Program has worked with 19 promising youngsters who have won over 50 awards for first place at various regional and national golf championships in the country.

Social Sustainability

DLF Cyberhub, the iconic food and beverage destination in Gurugram, witnessed an initiative in partnership with Zomato Feeding India, where food was distributed to more than 15,000 needy people during the Covid-19 pandemic. The initiative helped feed thousands of people in need and was one of the largest food distribution drives in the country.

In line with the idea of community-driven development, DLF Foundation works towards the holistic development of communities. DLF Foundation has taken up initiatives to provide safe drinking water and promote cleanliness in various public spaces. In addition, DLF Foundation has also taken up the task of providing access to quality healthcare to communities, especially in remote and underdeveloped areas of the country.

Healthcare

Healthcare is a crucial area of intervention for DLF Foundation and there are multiple on ground initiatives and Projects in this area which are implemented in partnership with the leading healthcare providers and hospitals.

Facing the Covid-19 Pandemic, DLF Foundation established two fully equipped and staffed Covid Care Facilities in partnership with “NH Narayana Super Specialty Hospital” and an NGO, “Care India” that have treated Covid-19 infected patients since 2021. Understanding the importance of vaccination as the key to beating this pandemic, DLF Foundation has partnered with leading Indian hospitals and has so far vaccinated over 30,000+ persons across 5 states. Medical Helplines were started in partnership with Medanta and NH Narayana Super Specialty Hospital during the Covid-19 pandemic to provide online medical consultation with leading doctors.

\

DLF Foundation also provided COVID Medical and Safety Equipment to Government Bodies and Hospitals across several states to aid in the fight against the Covid pandemic. Medical and safety equipment including CT Scan machine, ventilators, face masks, PPE kits, oxygen concentrators, oxygen cylinders and Hospital Beds were provided to district administrations of Delhi, Gurugram, Noida, Uttarakhand, Chennai, Bengaluru, Lucknow, Panchkula and Indore.
